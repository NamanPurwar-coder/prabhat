About the Department

The department teaches Chemistry, English, Mathematics, and Physics in the B.Tech programs. Experienced faculty provide foundational knowledge to prepare first-year B.Tech students in these subjects, focusing on applied approaches.

Vision

We aim to apply our unique values to undergraduate programs, preparing individuals to thrive and contribute to a global society by building a strong foundation for excellence in applied sciences and technology.

Mission

To awaken young minds through dedication to teaching, commitment to students, and the use of innovative instructional methods like PPTs and visual aids.

HOD Desk Message

The Applied Science & Humanities department is focused on imparting fundamental knowledge required in all engineering streams. With experienced faculty and well-equipped labs, we are committed to providing strong foundational skills to students in subjects like Chemistry, Mathematics, and Physics.

Dr. Rakesh Singh

HOD, ASH

Practical Exposure

Experiments for first-year students include Spectrometer, Laser Diffraction, Ammeter-Voltmeter Calibration, Transistor Characteristics, and Carey Foster's Bridge.
