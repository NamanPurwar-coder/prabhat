Careers at Prabhat Engineering College

Established in 2008 and affiliated with AKTU, PEC Kanpur is a top institution for Engineering, MBA, and Polytechnic education. Our mission is to develop skilled professionals through modern education and infrastructure.

🎓 Programs We Offer

MBA

  • Specializations: HR, Marketing, Finance, IT
  • Seats Available: 60
  • Approved by AICTE

B.Tech

  • Computer Science – 120 seats
  • Electrical – 45 seats
  • Mechanical – 45 seats
  • Civil – 30 seats

Polytechnic

  • Branches: Civil, Mechanical, Production, Electrical
  • Seats: 60 per branch

🏫 Campus Facilities

  • Wi-Fi enabled campus and digital classrooms
  • Fully-equipped labs and workshops
  • Library with DELNET access
  • Separate hostels for boys and girls
  • Indoor gym, sports facilities, mess and cafeteria

👨‍🏫 Our Faculty

With an average academic experience of 10 years and a faculty-student ratio of 1:6, our educators are dedicated to delivering high-quality, personalized instruction across all disciplines.
