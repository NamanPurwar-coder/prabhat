About the Department

This is a period of glory for the Computer Science and Engineering profession as the rapidly changing technology creates many opportunities and challenges. Department of Computer Science and Engineering is prepared to meet the challenges and is playing a leadership role in shaping the education of the 21st Century by providing unique educational and research opportunities in the forefront of Computer Science and Engineering.

The students are focused with the use of conceptual understanding of core domain areas in computing as well as enhanced programming skills disseminating their analytical abilities. Our aim is to provide our students the lifelong learning and leadership skills that enable them to grow in their professions and advance to positions of responsibility by effective Industry-Institute Interaction. The department has an excellent placement record and our students are placed in many prestigious software industries and MNCs.

Vision

To become a leader in providing high quality education and research in the area of Computer Science, Information Technology, and allied areas.

Mission

To develop human resources with theoretical and practical knowledge in the discipline of Computer Science and the ability to apply the knowledge for the benefit of society at large.
