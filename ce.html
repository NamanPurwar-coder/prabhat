About the Department

The Civil Engineering Department is committed to producing technically competent civil engineers who can contribute to nation-building through quality education, research, and consultancy. Our faculty is dedicated to providing students with a strong foundation in engineering principles and practical applications.

Vision

To be a leader in the field of Civil Engineering by imparting quality education in developing highly competent manpower and by conducting research and consultancy in nation-building.

Mission

  • To impart knowledge and to equip students with skills to prepare them for successful diverse professional careers.
  • To develop innovative and simple instructional materials for the benefit of students.
  • To benefit humanity, nation, and society through research, creativity, problem-solving, and application development.
  • To empower practicing engineers with state-of-art technology to meet the growing challenges of the field.
  • To develop linkages with leading R&D organizations and educational institutions in India and abroad for excellence in teaching, research, and consultancy practices.

HOD Desk Message

Welcome to the Civil Engineering Department! We are committed to providing our students with a strong academic foundation and practical exposure in structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, and transportation engineering. Our department encourages students to engage in research and community services to address societal challenges effectively.
