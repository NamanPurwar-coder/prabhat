Fee Structure

At Prabhat Engineering College, we believe in making quality education affordable and accessible. Our fee structure is thoughtfully designed to ensure that students receive top-tier education without financial burden.

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Our B.Tech program offers excellent infrastructure, expert faculty, and strong industry connections. The fees are structured to deliver maximum value for your investment in engineering education.

Polytechnic

The Polytechnic program is ideal for students looking for a practical, skill-based approach. We offer competitive fees with a focus on hands-on training and employability.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Our MBA program is designed for future leaders and entrepreneurs. With a fee structure that emphasizes value for money, this program blends academic rigor with real-world application.

*For detailed fee inquiries, please contact our admission office or visit the campus. Personalized counseling and flexible payment plans available.
