About the Department

Electrical Engineering encompasses Power Engineering, Control Systems, Signal Processing, and Instrumentation. The department offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs to equip students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in these areas.

Vision

To produce excellent Electrical & Electronics Engineering professionals with a strong emphasis on social and environmental concerns, supported by state-of-the-art research facilities.

Mission

To induct high-caliber students and provide them with a conducive learning environment, emphasizing technical expertise, practical skills, and industry-oriented projects.

HOD Desk Message

Electrical Engineering is at the forefront of technological advancements, encompassing power systems, control systems, and instrumentation. Our department focuses on producing competent graduates ready to address contemporary challenges in power distribution, automation, and smart grid systems.

Mr. Pradeep Singh

HOD, EE Department
