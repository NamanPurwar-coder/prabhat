Message from the Training & Placement Cell

Prabhat Engineering College is committed to creating top-tier professionals. Our legacy and results stand as testimony to our promises. We believe that every student is unique and has distinct needs. Our mission is to provide them with a high-quality platform to nurture their talents and grow holistically.

The quality of education a student receives significantly impacts their future. A value-based education and a strong career foundation are both essential and a right for every student. At PEC, we strive to impart cutting-edge technical skills through classroom teaching and co-curricular activities, ensuring the all-around development of our students.

Beyond academics, we offer sports, motivational sessions, and inspirational programs to support the personal growth of our students. We treat our students like family, preparing them to meet global challenges with confidence and excellence.

Recently, we conducted several workshops to keep students up-to-date with the latest technologies. This year's placement season was a milestone—students received multiple job offers, with placement rates ranging from 100% to 400%. Renowned companies visited our campus and selected some of the brightest minds.

Encouraged by the enthusiastic response from both industries and students, we have designed our placement calendar for the 2019–20 session with even more opportunities. We deeply value the aspirations of our students and their parents. At PEC, we remain committed to providing the best career platforms for our students.

We welcome parents to trust us with their children’s future and select PEC as a launchpad for a successful career. We wish all our students the very best in their future endeavors.
